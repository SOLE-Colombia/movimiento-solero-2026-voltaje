# Boost your mobile signal

Hi, I'm Sanjay and I welcome you to SOLE Voltage. ## What is amplifying your mobile signal inside a space?

Amplifying the mobile signal of your cell phone means that, with some adjustments, you can increase the strength of the signal received and emitted by your phone, which improves the quality and reliability of the connection. 

## What is it good for?

Are you not being heard, or are you being cut off when you talk on the phone? Are you going to do a SOLE and the pages are not loading or the search is slow? You can look for a stronger signal for your phone to get better service and speed up your SOLE sessions. Here are some simple tricks to improve your mobile connection at home and facilitate collaborative learning in your community.

## **What do you need?**

- Cell phone
- Mobile data connection

## **How to do it?**

- **Step 1: Change room or room**
    
    If you are at home, try different rooms to find the best signal. Consider the direction of the cell tower and building materials that could affect the connection. Find the optimal location to boost the signal.
    
- **Step 2: Remove your phone case**
    
    Remove any barriers that may be affecting your signal. Some poorly designed cases block the antenna, so remove the case and see if reception improves. Sometimes simplicity is the solution.
    
- **Moment 3: Turn Airplane Mode On and Off**
    
    Give your phone a deep breath. Forcing network scanning by turning airplane mode on and off can be helpful, especially after changing locations. This step helps your phone automatically select the best available signal.
    

## **How do I know if this solution works?**

By following these steps, you are resetting and improving your mobile connection settings. This will not only enhance your browsing experience, but also allow you to share this signal with other devices during your SOLE sessions.

## **Why would this solution work?**

Restarting the connection is a great step to improve the speed and stability of your signal. However, keep in mind that the signal quality also depends on the coverage in your geographical area - these tricks will allow you to browse the Internet faster, but the area must have good carrier coverage!

Remember, for SOLE-savvy communities, having a solid connection is crucial. Learning to share the Internet is an important step for future computer moments, facilitating group learning with larger screens - sharing the connection is the first step towards an even more enriching SOLE! 📶✨

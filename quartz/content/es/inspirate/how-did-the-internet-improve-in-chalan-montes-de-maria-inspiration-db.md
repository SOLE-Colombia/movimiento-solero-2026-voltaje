Chalán is a municipality in the department of Sucre, Colombia. In this region, villages like Monte Bello, Rancho Rojo, and Alemania are using the internet in unique ways: connecting as a group to answer big questions, complete homework, watch movies, or simply share their stories with the world.

Since 2023, **Yulieth Farasica** has been a SOLE Ambassador who has driven significant change in her community by leading collaborative learning sessions. Additionally, this year she helped us install, teach, and promote internet sharing in her village, Alemania. Now, neighboring villages can also connect to the internet, improving communication and access to information.

This is how Alemania is now a meeting point for Monte Bello, Rancho Rojo, and other nearby communities. People now travel to use this connection, which has been installed and operational thanks to everyone's efforts.

[Gabriel, Vistas de Sintonización, Montes de María, (2024).](../../../How did the internet improve in Chalán, Montes de  1f408291a419462989817363aac27111/20240710_SOLEWLH_Sintonizacin_MM_Chaln_(10).mp4)

Gabriel, Vistas de Sintonización, Montes de María, (2024).

Six ambassadors have now been trained in the SOLE methodology and have learned how the internet works and how to use their Jangala to share it with others!

Yulieth, Astrid, Yuranis, Johana, Celia, Maryuris, and Yolanda are changing the world. Thanks to their work, more children, youth, adults, and seniors in Chalán now have access to the internet. From neighboring villages like Monte Bello and Rancho Rojo, people come to Vda. Alemania to connect and become part of this network, which they themselves have created and sustained.

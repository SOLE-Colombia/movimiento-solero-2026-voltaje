# A powerful message can change the world, connect with your purpose

## Welcome

Hello! I'm Manu, the storyteller of SOLE Colombia and today I want to talk to you about the power of a message.

If you are here it's because you want to learn how you can mobilize others with your cause and build a powerful message. Shall we do it together?

## What is creating a powerful message?

In this solution, we are going to dive into a very special methodology in the world of storytelling: the Golden Circle, created by Simon Sinek. We will use this step-by-step methodology to help you develop a clear and powerful message.

Through the three fundamental questions posed by Sinek, I will guide you to unravel the message behind your cause. When you convene others with a clear and powerful message you make them feel deeply connected to you. 

Get ready because this is going to be exciting!

## What's the point?

Creating a powerful, purpose-driven message means connecting emotionally with your audience and inspiring them to take action. It serves to inspire others, appeals to their emotions and values creating a deeper and more meaningful connection. You will create a more coherent and consistent communication.

## **What do you need?**

- Paper.
- Stylus/Pencil or any digital tool.
- And, of course, enthusiasm.

## How to do it?

[Vídeo sin título ‐ Hecho con Clipchamp (5).mp4](Crea un mensaje potente 12a2bd68c5b680c198cee71d7d6322e7/Vdeo_sin_ttulo__Hecho_con_Clipchamp_(5).mp4)

- **Moment 1: Start with the what**
    
    What do you do or want to do, what do you want to share or offer to the world? That is the "what" of your message. Starting with the what will help you to glimpse more easily your why.
    
    Here your ideas take shape and become tangible actions: a product, a service or an offer. 
    
    **Activity**: To discover it, I invite you to write down on a blank sheet of paper what you are doing or offering in tangible terms... A SOLE? an activity? a product?
    
    If you still have doubts, use the examples to start your golden circle. Courage!
    
    [Vídeo sin título ‐ Hecho con Clipchamp (6).mp4](Crea un mensaje potente 12a2bd68c5b680c198cee71d7d6322e7/Vdeo_sin_ttulo__Hecho_con_Clipchamp_(6).mp4)
    
- **Moment 2: Define the how**
    
    We are on the right track! Now that you know the flame that moves you, it's time to think about how to fan it and take your message to the world. What strategies, actions or methods will you use to make your why shine?
    
    It's time to determine the methods and processes you will use to bring your "what" to life. For this step you need to know *how you do what you do* to fulfill your motivation. Think about whether it is a format **that makes you unique**, a product that **no one else has thought of**, or an **original** service you offer. What matters here are the qualities
    
    **Activity:**
    
    1. Write on a sheet of paper a list of the activities you do to fulfill your "what" and select the ones you find most relevant. 
    2. Now put them all in the same place and write your how.
    
    **See how I did it to guide you:**
    
    [Vídeo sin título ‐ Hecho con Clipchamp (7).mp4](Crea un mensaje potente 12a2bd68c5b680c198cee71d7d6322e7/Vdeo_sin_ttulo__Hecho_con_Clipchamp_(7).mp4)
    
- **Moment 3: Shape your why.**
    
    Finally, you need to be clear about the fundamental purpose behind the message. **What is the inspiring reason that motivates you?** 
    
    When we are clear about our "why", we create an emotional bond with our audience, awakening their interest and commitment from a deeper level.
    
    **Activity: To discover the why of your message, grab a blank sheet of paper and write down what first comes to mind. Don't hesitate too long.** It's okay if it's just a few words, little by little it will take shape 😉That's my why:
    
    [Vídeo sin título ‐ Hecho con Clipchamp (8).mp4](Crea un mensaje potente 12a2bd68c5b680c198cee71d7d6322e7/Vdeo_sin_ttulo__Hecho_con_Clipchamp_(8).mp4)
    
    Tip: **Think about what it is that motivates you to do what you're doing, and you'll see that little by little the words will appear.**
    

## How do you know if this solution works?

There you have it, you have your why ready and so your message is much more powerful. Now you can have more clarity when you want to infect others with your purpose or that of your product. Remember, the most important thing is that it is authentic, that it reflects your essence and that it makes you feel identified.

Now is your time to shine! Go ahead, conquer the world with your message! 🚀 Show us in the comments what your Golden Circle looks like!

## Why might this solution work?

Your message should reflect your purpose, that thing that drives you to action. It must be authentic and resonate with the people who hear you.

Aligning your messages and your actions with your purpose makes your communication coherent, take care of it. If your message is genuine, authentic and reflects your true passion, you will be honoring your why. 

It is very important that your message creates an emotional connection with whoever receives it, remember that only when we create emotional bonds with others, we get the words to truly touch the hearts of others. 

Remember! the goal of a powerful message is to motivate others to do something, to take action and follow you, to inspire, connect and motivate them to follow your vision.

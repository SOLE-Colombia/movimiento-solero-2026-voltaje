# Making a small antenna for my cell phone

## **Welcome**

It's a pleasure to greet you, my name is Mateo and I will accompany you in the development of this solution. If you are reading, listening or watching this is because you are interested or curious about how to improve the signal of your cell phone with a tin. If so, you are in the right place.

This solution can take you an estimated time of **30 to 40 minutes** and has a **basic level of difficulty.**

## **What is this solution for?**

This solution is used to temporarily improve the signal of your cell phone. However, the quality of this signal will depend on the weather or the conditions of the place where you are, your operator and the number of towers that emit radio waves around you so that you can make calls or surf the Internet, so the success of this solution will depend on other factors. However, this solution is a simple, fast and very interesting option to contribute to improve your cell phone signal. What we can tell you, for now, is that the results of this test will be exciting and revealing. 

## **What do you need?**

- One person to work on this solution is enough. Cheer up.
- A clean metal can, it can be a beverage can.
- A scissors.
- Scalpel (optional).
- A ruler, preferably metallic.
- A fine-tipped marker.
- Thick transparent tape.
- Safety gloves.

## **Before we get started, a few safety issues!**

Although this solution has a basic level of difficulty, it is important that you keep in mind some recommendations for your safety:

- Work on a flat, rigid surface, preferably a table that you can cut on.
- Look for a clean can that is not rusty.
- We recommend you to be in a quiet place, without distractions and to have as much time and patience as possible.
- Keep your hands at a safe distance from the cuts you will make with the scissors or scalpel.
- Preferably wear safety gloves.
- We recommend you to be seated in a comfortable place to work.

## How to do it?

This solution has 3 moments, but don't worry, you are not alone, I will accompany you on this journey. Keep in mind that some steps can be frustrating and others very exciting, and that's ok, it's part of tinkering. The important thing is to keep trying.

- **Step 1: Draw a metal foil**
    1. Prepare all the materials mentioned above.
    2. To perform this step we will need the can, the scissors and the scalpel. Place the beverage can on a flat and rigid surface, preferably on a table. Cut the can with the scissors or the scalpel in such a way that an open sheet is left, imagine that it is a rectangular sheet of paper. Remember to do this step with patience, cut the can on top of the flat surface you are working on and place your hand at a safe distance from the cut you are going to make.
    3. Once you have cut the can like a rectangular sheet of paper, flatten it to eliminate as many irregularities as possible on the surface. Think of it as a smooth sheet of paper. This will be the metal sheet we will work with from now on.
    4. With the ruler and the fine tip marker, draw a 4 cm by 4 cm square on the metal sheet. *I care about your safety, so it is important to be careful with all the cuts you are going to make and remember to preferably wear safety gloves.*

- **Step 2: Cut a 4 x 4 cm square.**
    1. Cut the 4 cm x 4 cm square with the scissors. With this you will have a square metal sheet.
    2. Choose one of the sides of this square metal sheet and mark with a fine tip marker the center of the side you chose, that is, 2 centimeters.
    3. Place the ruler in the center you previously marked and mark with the fine tip marker 0.5 cm on each side of that center. Repeat this same step on the opposite side of the side you are working on. Join the marks and draw the lines so that you have a 1 cm rectangle. *How does it feel? If you finished step 7, we are on the right track. If not, we invite you to repeat the steps with patience. Here I will wait for you.*
    4. On each of the two drawn lines, draw marks every 0.5 cm. As a result you should have 7 marks on each of the lines.

- **Step 3: Install your antenna on the cell phone**
    1. Draw from side to side lines perpendicular to the marks you made in the previous step. Then, cut these lines drawn on each side without cutting the central column that has the width of 1 cm.
    2. For this step you should have as a result 8 tabs cut on each side of the metal sheet. For each side bend the intermediate tabs: the second, the fourth, the sixth and the eighth. Now cut each of the remaining bent tabs. *If you've reached this step, you should have a piece of metal with 4 tabs on two sides.*
    3. Almost there! We are very close to finishing. For this step you should line your metal piece on both sides with thick transparent tape, this will allow you to protect it.
    4. Finally, with transparent tape stick this metal piece on the back right side of your cell phone (where your cell phone antenna is usually located) and protect it, preferably, with the cover of the cell phone you are using. *If you made it to this step, I send you all my congratulations and hope this journey has been challenging and exciting.*

## **How to know if this solution works?**

- You can test this solution by checking the signal bars or dots on your cell phone, which are usually located in the upper left or right corner of your screen. These bars measure how strong a signal you are receiving. In theory, the more bars you have, the better signal you should be receiving.
- Another option is to test the download speed on your cell phone. Depending on the device you have you can download an application to monitor this speed and see, in different conditions, if the download speed is indeed improving.
- This solution can achieve a significant improvement in the signal reception of your cell phone in one or maximum two bars or points. Note that the signal may not improve in places where there has never been a signal.

## **Why would this solution work?**

Keep in mind that there is your cellular signal with which you can make calls and send text messages; and the Wi-Fi signal with which you can connect to the internet. By placing the can on the back right side of your cell phone, these signals can be amplified because the can is placed on top of the antenna inside the cell phone; amplifying and redirecting the signal. The key is that you can test this can in different conditions and draw your own conclusions as to whether or not this solution is working.

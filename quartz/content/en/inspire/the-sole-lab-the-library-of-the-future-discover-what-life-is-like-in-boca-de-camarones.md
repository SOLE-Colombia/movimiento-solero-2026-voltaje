Not everyone has a public space with connectivity for SOLE, for learning to learn in groups using the internet, or for patching around the internet!

Today, we are happy to share with you that we were able to build this dream space. See what the Library of the Future is all about, and what it's like in the community of Boca de Camarones, where together we built our first SOLELab.

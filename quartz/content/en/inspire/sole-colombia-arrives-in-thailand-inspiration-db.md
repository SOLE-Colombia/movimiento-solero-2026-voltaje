We flew more than 40 hours ✈️🌏 to bring Self Organized Learning (SOLE) to more places around the world. 🌎💡 In Myanmar, education is taking another direction with this methodology that empowers children, youth and adults👧👦 to learn by following their curiosity. 💭

In rural communities 🏞️, where access to traditional education 📚 can be limited, SOLE is creating new opportunities to discover the world 🌐🔍 and develop essential skills for the future.

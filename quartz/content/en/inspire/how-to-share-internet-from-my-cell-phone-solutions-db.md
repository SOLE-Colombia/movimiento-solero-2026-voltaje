# **Restart and share your phone's internet with others**

## Welcome

Hello! This solution was written by Nicolás, one of the SOLE Colombia tinkerers.

It is reviewed by me, Belén, and although I like to tinker, sometimes I find it hard to understand how things work. I guess I'm already a few years old and everything changes very fast... when I started using computers, or computers, they were basically typewriters. But not anymore... I encourage you to lose your fear!

## What is setting up a personal WiFi zone?

Did you know that your smart phone has an almost permanent Internet connection? Usually with cell phone plans, a data plan is attached, which allows you to access the Internet. 

That connection can become slow or inefficient and you have to restore it. But you might also want to share it with others, want to know how? Read on.

## **What is it for?**

You may want to share the Internet signal of your data plan with other people. But you may also find that, even if you have an Internet connection, the pages you want to open load with difficulty, or the data call connection does not work well.

Almost always, the best way to get everything back up and running is to restart everything from scratch. 

Once rebooted, you will be able to share your phone's Internet signal to make SOLE with your community!

## **What do you need?**

- A cell phone.
- Enough data on your cell phone to share with others.
- Generosity.

## **How to do it?**

- **Step 1: Restart your data connection.**
    
    Swipe down from the top of your phone to access the control panel. Once here, select the **"Mobile data"** option **,** turn it off for a few seconds and turn it back on. Wait a moment for the phone to access the cellular network and that's it! Your connection will be fresh and working properly.
    
    
    Want to know another trick? Activate the airplane mode, this will reset all your phone's settings related to the Internet signal.

- **Moment 2: Set up the mobile hotspot**
    
    Go to settings and look for **"mobile hotspot",** in English it appears as **"Hotspot".**
    
    In this menu look for the option **"Set up a mobile hotspot".** You will see several data, for now we are only interested in configuring the name of the [WiFi network](/wifi-fidelidad-inalambrica-wifi-wireless-fidelity-conceptorio) you create and the access password. 
    
    Once you change them, click on the pimp (or the cross) to save the new configuration.

- **Step 3: Share internet**
    
    Slide the top of your phone down to access the control panel, you can find the option **"Mobile hotspot",** **"Hotspot",** or something similar.
    
    Simply activate it to share the Internet signal with whomever you want. 
    
    If you want to do SOLE, this is an excellent solution to get all computers to perform their search. You know that in SOLE Colombia we are always thinking about how to take advantage of the resources that we have individually to take our communities to improve their Internet experience... there is nothing like using it as a group!

## **How to know if this solution works?**

With this recipe you can reset your phone's cellular network settings. This will improve browsing speed and connection stability. You will be able to share it with other phones and/or computers and search the Internet for answers to the Big Questions that motivate your SOLE.

## **Why would this solution work?**

Restarting the cellular network connection will improve your connection and you will be able to surf a little faster, but the geographical area where you are must have coverage from your operator. 

This recipe will not work if the phone signal in your area is not of good quality. Learning to share the Internet will be a very important step for the moment you get computers, since it is easier to use the Internet in a group with large screens.

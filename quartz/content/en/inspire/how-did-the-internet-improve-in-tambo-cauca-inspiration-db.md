**Have you ever imagined a place where an entire community could connect to the Internet, learn and create together?** In El Tambo, Cauca, that dream was a shared need.

In this community, Internet connection in rural areas is very limited, and in the urban area, access is mostly private. A public space with Internet became essential for everyone to connect, work on their collective projects and strengthen their territory.

In 2023, thanks to our **ADAPT Peace Building** partners, we met the community of El Tambo and their community circles: the youth circle, the water circle and the women's circle. These groups had been working for more than a year on peace building and strengthening the social fabric. From that first meeting, a deep connection was created. The circles quickly appropriated the SOLE methodology and saw the group Internet as a tool to achieve a collective dream: a community space with Internet connection. This is how the spark of the **SOLE Lab** was ignited in this community.

During 2024, the Ambassadors of El Tambo were trained in the SOLE methodology and, together with **RUTA 4 Taller**, the collective work and the hands of the community gave life to their own SOLE Lab: The **Second Library of the Future in Colombia**!

The SOLE Lab is not an ordinary library. It is a meeting point where children, youth and adults explore, create, dream and learn together. With community Internet access and collaborative spaces, the SOLE Lab invites the community to turn ideas into actions, make collective decisions, enjoy self-organized group learning and, why not, even watch movies.

Thanks to the SOLE Lab, the Internet connection in El Tambo has improved significantly. Today, anyone can come and enjoy a more stable and faster connection, allowing students, families and neighbors to connect, research and create together new ways to use the Internet. 

Now the El Tambo community can communicate and create links with other SOLE Laberas communities and people around the world. With each connection, bridges of collaboration are built that strengthen the sense of belonging and collective learning, each participant in the Library of the Future is part of a global network of learning and transformation.

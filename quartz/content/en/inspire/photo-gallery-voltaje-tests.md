The Voltaje tests have helped us explore, evaluate, and further develop the solutions and devices we've developed to awaken the tinkering spirit, foster an understanding of technology and connectivity, and lead us to create spaces for autonomous and communal learning.

Thanks to all the people who have participated with us in these tests, we've been able to continue creating ways to extend the internet to many places and communities. In addition, we've been able to better understand what we do at Voltaje.

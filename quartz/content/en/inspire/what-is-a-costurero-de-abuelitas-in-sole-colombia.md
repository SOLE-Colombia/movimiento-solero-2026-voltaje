SOLE Colombia has a group of volunteers around the world who connect with SOLE sessions to support participants, encourage them to explore new questions related to what they're interested in, and have fun and learn together. This group is called [La Nube de Abuelitas](/la-nube-de-abuelitas-como-cambiar-el-mundo-desde-tu-casa-inspiration-db).

When you tell someone that we're calling for volunteers to be part of La Nube de Abuelitas and that the moment we tell them what this volunteer program is about is a *Grandmas Sewing Kit*, the same questions always arise: *Why is it called a Sewing Kit? What is a Sewing Kit?* And, I have to confess, I almost enjoy keeping secret the reason that led us to choose that name.

In Colombia, the word "costurero" has two meanings. The first refers to the box that houses sewing supplies, such as thread, thimbles, scissors, needles, and more. The second refers to **that moment when people who sew gather**. At that moment, people sew, knit, and also talk. They talk about things that are happening, about life, about the people we love, and about the things we learn. It's the place where decisions are made, where encouragement and advice are given to those who need it, where everyone's achievements are celebrated, and where those who stumble are embraced. And both are very appropriate for Grandmas, don't you think?

Who doesn't imagine a Grandma mending dresses or hearts, or creating the lace for a blouse with her hands, or weaving the ties of a community. That's what we do: **The Cloud of Grandmas is a place to weave relationships, to weave our lives, and to strengthen the bonds that unite us. Each one weaves her patch and we all weave the plots that connect us remotely.

At a *Grandmas Sewing Room*, the first step is getting to know each other. We also reveal some details about the SOLE Colombia project we'll be participating in: the dates, the communities we'll be covering, the goal of our work, and the volunteer work we'll be doing. We explain what the trip will be like, who we'll be SOLEing with, and how we'll be working together.

We also share the tools we have to master the art of "SOL-ing grandparenting": the Grandmas' Manual, the SOLE Colombia Manual, the Loomio platform, the Grandmas' Cloud on WhatsApp, and the Registration Form so we can invite anyone we want. These are the essentials to be part of La Nube de Abuelitas.

We even take a picture!

In each SOLE Colombia Grandmothers' Sewing Box, we sew the scraps of the great quilt of solidarity that is the Internet. We show each other patterns that have worked and create new ones. Each one contributes her knowledge and her color, and we enjoy creating an equitable and beautiful future together, one Big Question at a time.

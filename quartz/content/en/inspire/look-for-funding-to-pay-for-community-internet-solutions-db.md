## Welcome

Hi, I'm Sanjay and one of my big ventures is to make community use of the resources we have available. I like to tinker, yes, but I'm also interested in the ways we humans coordinate to live better together. 

## What is taking up a collection to pay for community internet?

This solution gathers ideas on how to collect money to pay for a community connection, that is, to get a community to agree to financially support an asset that will be given common use and enjoyment.

## What is it for?

Finding ways to organize ourselves to acquire the things that improve our living conditions is sometimes a complex task. 

If you know that you need Internet, but you don't know how to get the people in your community to follow your idea, to support you in the maintenance and sustainability of the contract, or you don't know what steps to follow to be able to do it together, in this solution you can find useful orientations.

## What do you need?

- A very clear idea of what you want to collect the money for.
- Charisma, words and people skills to convince people to participate in the collection.
- Order to keep the accounts, handle the money and inform the people who collaborate with you of the course of the collection and the status of the accounts.
- Communication skills to inform the collaborators of the status of the service, maintenance needs or incidents. Also to receive suggestions or process the formalities required for the formalization or maintenance of the service.

## How to do it?

These recommendations are relevant when collecting money for community purposes. They are not necessarily followed in order, nor are they the only ones, but we believe they can help you.

- **Moment 1: Let's put together a team, it's easier to do it together.**
    
    **Look for partners** to start this campaign. 
    
    When we undertake projects for a common good, it is easy to get allies to distribute responsibilities. When you do this type of initiative alone, you can quickly get tired and give up, but with others this project will be more bearable.
    
    **Define who will be in charge of what**, this will make the work more organized and you will be able to follow up more easily.
    
- **Moment 2: Give a purpose to the collection that is clear and that excites you.**
    
    It is important to have the time to reflect and project what we want to do, what is it that we want to achieve? Remember, money is only a means to reach something bigger and more important... that is called purpose.
    
    In this case, **what is it that unites us in this collection, that inspires us to move to raise the funds?** When you are establishing the purpose of a project it is time to tirelessly ask ourselves what *for*, until we are convinced.
    
    [Doing SOLE](/como-hacer-sole-tutorial-inspiration-db) is a great way to find a purpose for the fundraiser, besides paying for it together, you can learn, dialogue and create thriving communities, using the internet as a group. Paying for the internet together allows you to use it to do SOLE, or for anything you can think of!
    
    For example:
    
    - Why do we want internet in a public access place? So that people in my community can learn new things they need or want to learn, talk and dialogue with other people and communities in a Big Conversation, and take action in a self-organized way to live better together.
    - Why do we want people in our community to be able to learn together? So that it is not necessary to depend exclusively on learning spaces and people related to the educational system, to realize that anyone can learn anything, using the internet. Why in a group? Because going anywhere alone is a difficult path, on the other hand, when we do it together, it is better.
    - What do we want them to be able to converse through the internet for? Interacting with other people with the objective of learning or that others can be part of our life always makes us feel better. Learning together when there are other people who participate in our findings and with whom we can walk the path together, makes us feel that what we learn transcends and is bigger than us. If people connect with each other through the internet it is because we are mutually interested in each other and can accompany each other, we are not alone on the road... Want to connect with people around the world in your SOLE sessions? Check out the [SOLE Granny Cloud](https://www.solecolombia.org/comunidad)! They are volunteers from all over the world, who connect to SOLE sessions to accompany, provide warm encouragement, share, learn and have fun together.
    - Why do we want to help our community to flourish, so that we can all have a happier, fuller and tastier life!
    
    Does it make sense to have public access internet in our community? Of course!
    
- **Moment 3: Let's identify those who might be interested in participating.**
    
    Who among our neighbors would be interested in having community internet? What interest might they have in contributing monetarily to this project? Then we can translate our project to their possible interests.
    
    Here are some examples of SOLEros who have self-organized to learn together, using the internet:
    
    - Elainer, when he used SOLE for young people in his community to learn how to make video games.
    - Robinson, who used SOLE so that his community could find sustainable alternatives to traditional crops in his community.
    - Maria, when, through SOLE, she got her students to take responsibility for their own educational process and changed the way they researched and learned in her school.
- **Moment 4: A story, the best way to communicate an idea**
    
    Now that we know who we are going to target with our campaign, let's create a story. We recommend answering five fundamental questions: who, what, when, where and why. 
    
    Here is ours:
    
    
    In the community of La Piche, a delicious honey is grown. Everyone in the community works in trades related to the production of its delicious honey: some have honeycombs, others collect the honey, others sell it, some manufacture related products... 
    
    We are a group of young people interested in creating natural cosmetic products based on honey and beeswax, but we do not know how to start manufacturing these products, nor the tools we need, nor the permits we require or how we could market them, etc. We want to start with how can we make a honey and beeswax lipstick? 
    
    Using the Internet we could find answers to these and other Big Questions!
    
    The cheapest Internet plan is $120,000 COP, no one on the sidewalk could afford it. But we have thought that, making a group of 12 people or families, we could pay a monthly fee of $10,000 COP and put the broadband internet by fiber optics in a place of easy access for everyone, such as the nursing home, the community hall or Doña Adela's store, which is the business that is open most of the time and where we could all connect. The connection could be used by all of us, even the children on the sidewalk, to do their homework.
    
    So, if you are interested and can be part of this collection, join this list! We will soon be able to have internet for us! 
    
    All expenses and bill payments will be sent on the first of each month to a [***WhatsApp***](/whatsapp-wapp-conceptorio) group and we will be able to keep up to date with any new developments. 
    
    Will you join the cause?

    
- **Moment 5: Buy, communicate and organize the expenses**
    
    With the money collected you just have to make the purchase. 
    
    Remember that none of these contracts have permanence clauses, so if you are not satisfied with the service, you can cancel it and move to another operator.
    
    It is important to be organized with your accounts and communicate payments and any new developments or inconveniences immediately to everyone involved. 
    
    In this way, everyone can take co-responsibility for any decisions regarding this collection and can assume the responsibilities or consequences together.
    
    Be sure to create a flow of communication with your collaborators, so that you can communicate in both directions and quickly solve any problems that may arise.
    

## How do you know if this solution works?

With this solution, you manage to organize the neighbors towards a common good, embodied in the payment of a service, to take advantage of it collectively and make the best possible use of it.

It also improves the connectivity conditions of the place where we live, making it a better place to live.

## Why might this solution work?

Organizing ourselves to achieve things will get us as far as we want to go.

In this case, it allows us to have a shared internet and a public access point. It is important to measure very well the bandwidth offered by the service to hire. Thus, we will be able to hire a sufficiently fast and powerful service in relation to the number of people we want to link to that connection.

The next step would be how to improve the conditions of this connectivity, without a significant increase in costs.

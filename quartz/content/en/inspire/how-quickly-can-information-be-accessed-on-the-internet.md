We say the internet is fast if we can quickly access the information we're looking for.

Download megabytes (MB) are a measure of how quickly internet data is transmitted. The more MB you have, the faster your internet is.

**If you have:**

- **More than 5 MB:** This is a good speed, allowing you to watch videos, listen to music, and perform various internet activities.
- **Between 1 MB and 5 MB:** You can make stable video calls.
- **Less than 1 MB:** You can perform internet searches and send text messages.

*The more devices connected to the internet, the fewer MBs you'll download. This happens because the speed is distributed across the number of connected devices.*

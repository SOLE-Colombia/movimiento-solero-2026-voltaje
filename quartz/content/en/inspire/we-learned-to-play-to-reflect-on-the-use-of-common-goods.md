# We learned to play to reflect on the use of common goods

Did you know you can use the game to create community agreements? 💭

In the Fish Game, we learn through a simple but powerful experience: the more we catch for ourselves, the fewer fish there are for others. But when we work together, we make sure there's enough for everyone.

This game is a real-life metaphor. Fish aren't just fish. 🌱 They represent the resources we share: the internet, the land, food, even the air we breathe!

Think of it this way: imagine sharing an internet plan with your neighbors. If we all use it consciously, we can make it go further and benefit the entire community. But if we only think about our own interests, the resource is depleted quickly and harms everyone.

Are you ready to make agreements with your community?

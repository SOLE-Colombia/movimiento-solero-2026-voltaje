The Second Library of the Future in the Obrero neighborhood of Tambo is now a reality! ✨

Today, children, youth, and adults have a public space with an internet connection to create, learn, connect, share with others, and do whatever they want together in this space.

🧠💻 A space where ideas become action, where dreaming is manifesting. Congratulations, Tambo community! 🎊 Your SOLE Lab is waiting for you with open doors! 🔓

**A future with Voltage**

| Script  |
| --- |
| [Tension 1] **We live in a world where not everyone has internet and where we are increasingly isolated from each other.** However, in this world, there are communities in Colombia and Thailand that have come together to answer life's Big Questions using the internet as a group while building connectivity networks that have allowed them to improve their signal and strengthen their community fabric through SOLE Self-Organized Learning Environments. These communities are using community antennas, low-cost technologies, and a collaborative, caching spirit to overcome barriers and solve connectivity problems that once seemed insurmountable.  |
| [Tension 2] **This is what we do at SOLE Voltage, an internet solutions guide designed for the future**, but for a future where every internet connection is a vital connection between life stories that want to change the world together.  |
| [Tension 3] **We hear news and communications where the future is in a place that is not ours, it is as if the future does not belong to us, as if it is snatched from us or escapes us; we live in the promise of a future that we cannot decide.** But we no longer want to be spectators, we want to be the protagonists of our own history; we want to build our own future. We call this having a future with Voltage.  |
| [Voltage 4] **This is how our SOLE Voltage Solution Guide is also a Guide of Curiosity, for it does not simply offer easy answers;** instead, it poses questions that spark curiosity and empower people to find their own connectivity solutions while we create a banger community willing to support each other. This is Voltage, a statement that having internet we can be very lonely, but that, even without internet, we can be together to learn how to connect. |
| [Tension 5] **If not having the internet poses difficulties**, these communities show us that, with ingenuity and determination, it is possible to transform connectivity problems or challenges into opportunities. We use the internet in groups because it is safer and because we don't want to be alone. This is the future...no, sorry, this is our future! |
| [Tension 6] **If we feel isolated because we learned to use the internet alone,** we invite you to connect with these communities who are weaving a web of hope and solidarity to use the internet in groups. Their work is a powerful reminder that together we can build a more connected future, where every voice counts and every story finds its place (its place in your future); it is a reminder that another internet is possible. This new way of connecting is just beginning. We call this the Library of the Future. |
| [Tension 7] **In a corner of that future with Voltage** **that we are building, there are Libraries that do not depend on books, solitude and silence**, but on the joyful and lasting relationships between people who are learning to solve their connectivity problems. **This is a space where knowledge is not in a specific place**, but in the vast network of the internet and the human and non-human connections it enables.  |
| [Tension 8] This Library of the Future invites us to come together, to share ideas and to learn together. **We are not waiting for this future**; **this future does not depend on new technologies that arrive years later**; we are already building this future and it is materialized in the SOLE LAB that we built in Boca de Camarones, in Guajira...; in El Tambo, Cauca...; in Dominguillo, Santander de Quilichao..... SOLE LAB are community-based self-organized Learning Environments that have been designed on the strength of collaboration and trust: groups of people immerse themselves in deep discussions, using technology as a bridge to mutual understanding. |
| [Voltage 9] What if we imagine that future together with Voltage? Let's start building that future where every internet connection is a connection between life stories that want to build something together.  |

Is having internet access a problem for you? In a world where not everyone has internet access and where we feel increasingly alone, there are communities that have come together to answer life's Big Questions while building connectivity networks that have allowed them to improve their internet access and strengthen their community fabric through SOLE Self-Organized Learning Environments. These communities use community antennas, low-cost technologies, and a tinkering and collaborative spirit to overcome barriers and solve connectivity problems that previously seemed insurmountable.

What if it is there, in this desire to solve connectivity problems, where the future of the internet is born? What if every internet connection were a vital connection between life stories that come together to change the world together? We no longer want to be spectators, but protagonists of our own story.

> SOLE Voltage doesn't just offer easy answers; instead, it poses questions that spark curiosity and empower people to find their own solutions.
> 

These communities show us that, with ingenuity and determination, it is possible to transform connectivity problems or challenges into opportunities. We use the internet as a group because it is safer and because every internet connection is a connection with others, a bond of solidarity and learning.

So, let's celebrate these Colombian communities that are weaving a web of hope through the internet. Their work is a powerful reminder that together we can build a more connected future, where every voice counts and every story finds its place; it is a reminder that another internet is possible. This new way of connecting is just beginning. We call this the Library of the Future.

**SOLE Voltaje**

When someone faces a connectivity issue, SOLE Voltaje jumps in with its sparkling energy: “Have you tried restarting your router? It’s like giving your connection a coffee!” But its approach goes beyond simple tricks; it invites everyone to think: “Is your network overloaded because everyone is watching cat videos? What if you get together with other people to use the internet as a group? Maybe it’s time to come to an agreement about how we want to use the internet.”

**Library of the Future**

In a corner of the future, there is a Library that doesn't depend on books, but on the relationships between people. This is a space where knowledge isn't found in a specific place, but rather in the vast network of the internet and the connections it enables between people and communities. Here, life's Big Questions await exploration. This Library of the Future invites us to gather, share ideas, and learn together. In every corner, the energy of collaboration is felt: groups of people immerse themselves in deep discussions, using technology not as a barrier, but as a bridge to understanding. The Library of the Future teaches us that, through connection and dialogue, we can discover answers we hadn't even considered.

In a world where curiosity and technology converge, the Library of the Future has found an incomparable friend: SOLE Voltaje, a Solutions Guide designed to illuminate the path to knowledge. Together, they create a vibrant space where the internet is not just a tool, but an ally in the pursuit of life's Big Questions.

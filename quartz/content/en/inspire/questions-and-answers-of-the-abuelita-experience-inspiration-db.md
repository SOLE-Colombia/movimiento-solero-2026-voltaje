## What is the minimum amount of time I should commit to the sessions?

The key to learning is consistency. We understand that life and responsibilities change, and that's why some Grandmas won't be able to continue indefinitely. If you can't be involved continuously, or for an extended period of time, just let us know what you can do: it could be an occasional session or interacting with the team of coordinators, or other Grandmas, to support us with ideas on how to better face the challenges that arise.

## **How does seeing the differences in "privilege" between your environment and theirs affect us?**

Be confident in sharing your reality and your world with others: we are enriched, inspired, and explore possibilities when we come into contact with the realities of others.

This experience touches us all equally. You, too, discover and learn about other worlds. You, too, are enriched by this experience. Let this experience and these people enter your life!

## **What happens to the children or adults when the experience with La Nube de Abuelitas ends?**

It's a question many have asked, and it's a cause for concern. Although some experiences are shorter than expected, the impact it has on children and people in general is still present. Read our SOLE stories to find out what people are saying about La Nube de Abuelitas in the territories where we have accompanied SOLE sessions, so you can let yourself be touched by the magic.

## **What should I do if I am contacted by the media to talk about or film my interaction as part of La Nube de Abuelitas?**

If we are interacting with children, we must protect their interests and not expose them in an inappropriate way. Before speaking to the media or taking photos or videos of people, contact the SOLE Colombia team.

## **How is La Nube de Abuelitas funded?**

As we mentioned, the Grandmothers are volunteers, and thanks to this, many children, youth, and adults have been able to connect and share experiences. We want to keep it that way, although perhaps at some point we can explore new ways to maintain the relationship with the Grandmothers.

## **And if I can't be an Abuelita, is there any other way I can help?**

There are always opportunities to join this global movement to transform the future of learning. At SOLE Colombia, we are open to exploring the best way to connect. You can explore the roles of the SOLE Colombia Community ([[Comunidad](https://www.solecolombia.org/comunidad](https://www.solecolombia.org/comunidad))) and find where you feel most comfortable. Tell us what interests you or if you have a proposal!

**If you still have unresolved questions, write to us at [hola@solecolombia.org](mailto:hola@solecolombia.org); we're just a message away.**

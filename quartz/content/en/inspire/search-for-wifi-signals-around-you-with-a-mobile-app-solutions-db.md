## What is searching for WiFi signals around you with a mobile app?

Searching for WiFi signals with a mobile app involves using software on your smartphone to scan, analyze and find available wireless networks where you are. Many times, you will think that you have no Internet or that your mobile signal is poor (it depends on the service provided by your operator where you are), but, if you look closely, there may be some WiFi network with better connection or even free.

## **What is it for?**

Sometimes, the place where we are may not have ***Internet*** access, but if we move and go to a nearby place, we may find [WiFi](/wifi-fidelidad-inalambrica-wifi-wireless-fidelity-conceptorio) networks. Sometimes, even if we find them, we will not be able to access them because we do not know their respective keys. 

The [WiFi Map](https://www.wifimap.io/) application shows us a map with WiFi networks within a radius of 2 kilometers, according to the location of your device, and shares its access key. The networks saved in this application can be public or private.

If you want to make the most of a nearby connection, you can connect to those networks for group use using SOLE. You just need a few devices, four or five people eager to learn per device, and Great Questions to solve. Want to know more about how to do SOLE? See how to do it here: [¿Cómo hacer SOLE? - Tutorial](/como-hacer-sole-tutorial-inspiration-db) 

## **What do you need?**

- Cell phone
- Available space on your cell phone
- Mobile data connection
- Generosity

## **How to do it?**

- **Step 1: Install the app**
    - Before starting the installation process, you need to have a stable Internet connection.
    - Once you have found it, you can open the "Google Play" application for Android or Apple Store for IOS and search for the WiFi Map application.
    - Alternatively, you can access one of these links through a web browser:
        - https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.wifimap.wifimap&hl=es_CO
        - https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wifi-map-free-wifi-near-you/id548925969?l=es
- **Step 2: Configure the app**
    
    Once the app is installed, open it and accept the terms of use. Among them, it is very important that you accept the access to your location while the app is in use.

## **How to know if this solution works?**

With this recipe you can access multiple **WiFi** networks, both public and private. In cities, or urban centers, you will have a much better chance of finding networks with their access data available, compared to smaller population centers.

Now, with a Wifi network you can have more stability and speed to connect to the Internet, and why not to search for answers to your Big Questions, to learn, to take courses or to share the resource with others.

Are you ready to get started?

## **Why would this solution work?**

This solution will allow you to have temporary access to the Internet, mainly from population centers where users like you have shared access data to a public or private Internet network.

In order to access these keys you must have internet access, since only the paid application allows you to download the map with the access information.

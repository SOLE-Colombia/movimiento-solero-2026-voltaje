In 2023, the Dominguillo community began an unexpected transformation, thanks to a new opportunity for connection. It all began when, through the USAID *Threading Lives and Hope* program, implemented by the IOM, a device called *Jangala* arrived at the community public school library. This device provided, for the first time, stable internet access, which quickly became an invaluable resource for everyone.

Here, presenting the Jangala, or "Jang," as they decided to call him, is Luis Miguel Mina—or Lucho, as we all know him—one of the SOLE Ambassadors we trained in 2023:

The device was installed in the library to facilitate SOLE (Self Organized Learning Environments) sessions, a methodology that invites participants to learn autonomously and collaboratively through Big Questions. With these tools in place, Dominguillo members not only explored topics of interest but also began to answer questions about their culture and traditions, uniting the community around shared knowledge and rediscovering their roots through the internet.

Sin embargo, con el paso del tiempo la comunidad ya no pudo seguir utilizando la biblioteca del colegio para sus sesiones de SOLE . Para entonces, el impacto de esta conexión había sido tan profundo que los habitantes de Dominguillo no quisieron renunciar a ella. Convencidos de la importancia de tener un espacio comunitario para el aprendizaje y la conectividad, buscaron apoyo para crear su propio centro de conexión: el SOLE Lab de Dominguillo.

Y… ¿Adivinen qué? Juntos, trabajamos en el desarrollo del SOLE Lab, un espacio público en el que se instalaron sillas, mesas y computadoras y se restableció el acceso a Internet. Con el SOLE Lab, los habitantes de Dominguillo recuperaron la oportunidad de conectarse al mundo, continuar sus sesiones de SOLE y explorar nuevas formas de aprendizaje.

## What is La Nube de Abuelitas (The Spanish Granny Cloud)?

[The Granny Cloud](https://www.solecolombia.org/proyectos/nube-de-abuelitas)  is a concept born alongside the idea of SOLE and captures the spirit of those who accompany learning with a loving perspective, those who encourage us to learn in a subtle way, offering warm encouragement, asking pertinent questions, and being amazed by our findings.

They are people who have fun learning, are curious, and enjoy interacting with learners. Sugata Mitra called this perspective "Granny's perspective." Suneeta Kulkani, her research partner, formed The Granny Cloud, and at SOLE Colombia, we continue her legacy to form The Granny Cloud in Spanish.

## Who's in it?

We are volunteers, men and women, from around the world. We have Grandmas in Spain, Portugal, Denmark, Mexico, Colombia, the United States, Peru, Chile, Vietnam... there are Grandmas everywhere!

There is a Grandmas Cloud Coordinator, who is responsible for managing the sessions, scheduling and communicating the schedules and the people who will be attending each session requested by Grandmas.

Every year, coinciding with the start of the projects, new Grandmas join, discovering their motivation, and we train them in the methodology so they can SOLEar and enjoy this very rewarding role.

## How can you be an Abuelita?

What you need to be an Abuelita is a desire to connect with communities that learn through the SOLE Methodology. Do you want tools? You can download the [**Abuelitas Manual**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1VFjt21q6XTf6fwyChseL_r8bJCcGaINa/view) and find stories about Abuelitas on the [**SOLE Colombia website**](https://www.solecolombia.org/). You can also apply to join The Abuelitas Cloud through [**this form**](https://forms.gle/FZhW5h6QjSxvuPLW6). We will contact you with all the details.

Stay tuned to our social media and other platforms (check out our website) to find out when the next sewing workshop is held and the schedules for the next SOLEs. Don't miss out!

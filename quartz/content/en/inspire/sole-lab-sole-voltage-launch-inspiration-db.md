### Group 3

Authors:

Answer:

- **Mesh "Meshizo"**:

*Connect homemade antennas and routers at the highest points in the community, forming a self-expanding network, like a digital "spell" so that everyone is connected wirelessly. The magic of connectivity becomes reality!*

- **Pocket Internet**

*Send a cell phone or device to the village to download information in large quantities, like a "package" of knowledge that is then distributed among community members. The internet that travels in your pocket.*

- **Motocelular**

*A motorcycle picks up and delivers cell phones to the connectivity point and brings them back for a small fee.*

- **Starlink "Cosmic Cow"**:

*Use Starlink and fund it communally, with the collective contributions of residents (cows, chickens, and even the harvest!), creating a fund for "the internet that comes from space."*

- **Digital Palabrero**:

*Designate a trusted person as the "digital palabrero," who travels to a connection point and then returns to share the acquired knowledge, like a kind of "digital messenger."*

- **Digital Radio Clinic**:

*Create a digital clinic through local radio: people call with questions or concerns, and the "clinic" searches for answers on the internet, which are then transmitted back via radio. Connection through the airwaves and digital wisdom!*

- **Information Walkie Talkies**:

*Use walkie-talkies capable of transmitting short messages and data between connected points. A "digitized communication network" that transports bits and bytes without the need for internet access to every corner.*

- **The Computer CD**:

*Distribute information on CDs for those with older computers, creating a "mobile knowledge archive" that can be shared between people and read offline. The CD revolution, the disc that never goes out of style!*

- **Homemade Repeaters "The Connected Mango"**:

*Build homemade signal repeaters that travel weekly to the most remote locations (under the mango tree!), providing roaming internet access via small signal-boosting devices.*

- **Mobile Internet Provider**:

*Turn someone into a "mobile provider," a kind of operator that periodically brings internet access to different points in the community, connecting everyone as if it were a roaming internet network. Internet on the move!*

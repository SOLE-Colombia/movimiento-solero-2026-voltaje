How much data is used?

**Gigabytes (GB)** are the amount of data you can use for browsing, chatting, listening to music, watching videos, among other things. When you're offered an internet plan in GB, you're being offered the amount of internet data you can use.

**1 GB = 1000 MB (megabytes)**

**Do you know how many things you can do with 1 GB?**

- You can watch a 30-minute HD (high definition) video
- You can listen to 200 songs
- You can have 4 hours of video calls
- You can visit 600 websites
- You can send 350,000 text messages
